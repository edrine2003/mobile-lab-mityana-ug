⚠️ Seek Immediate Help If

🚨 Go to hospital immediately if you have:

Chest pain or difficulty breathing
Severe bleeding that won't stop
Loss of consciousness or fainting
High fever above 39°C (102°F)
Severe abdominal pain
Signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
Severe allergic reaction or swelling
Seizures or convulsions
🩹 Basic First Aid
1
🩸 Severe Bleeding
Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Do not remove the cloth. Elevate the injured area above heart level if possible. Call for help immediately.
2
💓 Someone Unconscious
Check for breathing. If not breathing, start CPR — 30 chest compressions then 2 rescue breaths. Continue until help arrives.
3
🔥 Burns
Cool the burn with running cold water for at least 10 minutes. Do NOT use ice, butter or toothpaste. Cover loosely with a clean bandage.
4
🤢 Poisoning / Overdose
Do NOT induce vomiting unless told by a doctor. Keep the person calm and awake. Bring the container/medicine to the hospital.
5
🦴 Broken Bone / Fracture
Do not try to straighten the bone. Immobilize the area with a splint or padding. Apply ice wrapped in cloth to reduce swelling.
6
🫁 Choking
For adults — give 5 back blows between shoulder blades, then 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver). For infants — use gentle back blows only.
