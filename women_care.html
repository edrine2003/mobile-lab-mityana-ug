🧼 Women's Care

Health, hygiene & wellness tips

🌿 Vulva Hygiene

Wash with clean warm water.
Avoid strong soaps or perfumes.
Wear breathable cotton underwear.
Avoid douching.

🩸 Menstrual Hygiene

  • Change pads regularly
  • Wash hands before changing pads
  • Dispose pads safely
  • Keep intimate area dry

💕 Healthy Lifestyle

  • Drink enough water
  • Exercise often
  • Eat fruits and vegetables
  • Sleep well
  • Manage stress

🚨 See A Doctor If You Notice

  • Bad smelling discharge
  • Severe pain
  • Persistent itching
  • Heavy bleeding
