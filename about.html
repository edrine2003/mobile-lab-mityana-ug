About Us

Mobile Lab Mityana is a digital laboratory service designed to make medical testing easier, faster, and more accessible. Our system allows patients to order laboratory tests online, receive results digitally, and communicate directly with laboratory professionals.

Our goal is to improve healthcare access by bringing laboratory services closer to the community, reducing waiting times, and ensuring accurate diagnostic services.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, fast, and accessible laboratory diagnostic services using modern technology while maintaining the highest standards of medical professionalism.

Our Vision

To become a trusted digital laboratory platform that connects patients and healthcare providers through efficient and innovative diagnostic solutions.

  • Malaria Testing
  • HIV Testing
  • Urine Analysis
  • Diagnostic Lab Reports
  • Online Result Delivery
  • Direct Communication with Lab Professionals
