🤰 Early Signs of Pregnancy

🩸 Missed Period

A delayed or missed period is one of the earliest signs.

🤢 Nausea

Morning sickness can occur during early pregnancy.

😴 Fatigue

You may feel tired more often than usual.

💧 Frequent Urination

Hormonal changes can increase urination frequency.

💖 Breast Tenderness

Breasts may feel sore, fuller, or sensitive.

🍟 Food Cravings

Some people experience cravings or food aversions.

💕 How To Improve Chances of Pregnancy

  • 🌸 Track ovulation and fertile days.
  • 🗓️ Have sex during the fertile window.
  • 🥗 Eat healthy foods rich in vitamins and iron.
  • 😴 Get enough sleep and reduce stress.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated and maintain healthy weight.
  • 🏃 Moderate exercise can improve fertility health.

⏰ Best Timing for Sex During Fertility

🌸 Most Fertile Time

The 5 days before ovulation and ovulation day are usually the most fertile.

💕 Frequency

Sex every 1–2 days during fertile window may improve chances.

🧘 Relaxation

Stress management may support overall reproductive health.

🌿 Physical Signs of Fertility

  • 💧 Clear stretchy cervical mucus
  • 🔥 Increased libido
  • 🌡️ Slight rise in body temperature
  • ⚡ Increased energy
  • 🌸 Mild pelvic discomfort during ovulation

🧼 Vulva & Feminine Hygiene

  • 🚿 Wash gently with clean water.
  • 🧴 Avoid harsh soaps and scented products.
  • ❌ Avoid douching.
  • 👙 Wear breathable cotton underwear.
  • 💧 Keep the area dry and clean.
  • 👩‍⚕️ See a doctor for itching, pain, or unusual discharge.
