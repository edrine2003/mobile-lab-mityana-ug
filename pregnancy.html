🌸 Common Early Signs

Missed period
Morning sickness
Breast tenderness
Frequent urination
Fatigue
Mood swings
Light spotting
Food cravings

🧪 When To Test

Take a pregnancy test after missing your period for more accurate results.

🚨 Visit a Clinic If You Have

  • Heavy bleeding
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • High fever
  • Dizziness
